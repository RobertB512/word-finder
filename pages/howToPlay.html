How to play

In this game, you form words by rearranging letters from a word that's given to you.

There are two levels in this game, easy and normal. In easy you can use each letter as many times as you want. In normal, you can only use each letter, at most, as many times as it appears in the word. Normal makes things more challenging, but also might make things more fun!

You can change your level or generate a new word at anytime, but be warned, by changing the level or generating a new word, your progress will be lost and a new word will be generated.

Examples

Want some examples on how to play? Look no further, you're here! If you want to skip right to the game, just scroll down and hit play!

Easy mode examples

Let's say the word is ostrich.

Valid word examples
  • rich (rich is a word)
  • hitch (there's a h)
  • roosts (ostrich has an o and s)
Invalid examples
  • rose (ostrich does not have an e)
  • it's (punctuation not allowed)

Normal mode examples

Let's say the word is crocodile.

Valid word examples
  • hits (hits is a word)
  • riot (riot is a word)
  • shirt (shirt is a word)
Invalid examples
  • I'd (punctuation not allowed)
  • decode (crocodile has one d and e)
  • crocodile (no free words)

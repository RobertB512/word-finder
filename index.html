Word Finder

Welcome to Word Finder. This is a game of finding words in an already existing word. How many can you find?

How to play

In this game, you form words by rearranging letters from a word that's given to you. Normally, from the way I played it at least, each letter can only be used as many times as it appears. In this game you can use each letter as many times as you want, as long it still forms a word of course.

Examples

invalid words
